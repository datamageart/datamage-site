Privacy notes

DATA/MAGE uses project intake details only to understand your inquiry, respond to you, and plan possible creative work together.

What you may send

The intake form may ask for your name, email address, project type, timeline, budget range, and a short project brief.

How it is used

Your information is used to reply to your inquiry, evaluate fit, prepare follow-up questions, and coordinate potential project work.

Sharing

DATA/MAGE does not sell inquiry details. Project details may be shared with collaborators only when needed to scope or deliver the requested work.

Retention

Inquiry details may be kept for follow-up and project records. You can ask for your inquiry details to be deleted when they are no longer needed.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, email booking@datamage.art.
